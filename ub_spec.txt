Comprehensive CRM for Universal Bank - Core Components
1. Lead & Customer Management Pipeline
Suspect → Prospect → Lead → Customer Journey:

Suspect Pool: Unqualified contacts from marketing campaigns, referrals, purchased lists
Prospect Qualification: Scoring system based on demographics, banking needs, assets, creditworthiness
Lead Nurturing: Automated workflows for different product lines (retail, SME, corporate, private banking)
Customer 360° View: Complete relationship overview across all products and touchpoints

2. Opportunity & Deal Management
Deal Pipeline Stages:

Identification → Qualification → Proposal → Negotiation → Closing → Post-sale
Product-specific pipelines (loans, mortgages, investment products, business accounts)
Deal scoring and probability tracking
Cross-sell/up-sell opportunity identification
Revenue forecasting and pipeline analytics
Approval workflows integrated with credit/risk systems

3. Activity & Task Management
Meeting Management:

Calendar integration (Outlook/Google)
Pre-meeting briefings with customer history
Meeting notes capture with automatic CRM updates
Follow-up task generation
Video meeting integration (Teams/Zoom)

Action Delegation & Tracking:

Task assignment with priority levels
Automated escalation for overdue tasks
Team workload balancing
SLA tracking for customer commitments
Mobile notifications for field staff

4. Target Management & Performance Tracking
Target Distribution:

Hierarchical target allocation (Branch → Team → Individual)
Product-wise and customer segment-wise targets
Territory/portfolio assignment
Dynamic redistribution based on performance

Target Tracking:

Real-time dashboards for achievement vs. targets
Product mix analysis
Conversion rate tracking by stage
Win/loss analysis
Incentive calculation automation

5. Access Control Architecture (4-Layer Model)
Layer 1 - Strategic (C-Suite/Executive):

Full system access
Bank-wide analytics and reporting
Strategic planning tools
Budget and target approval

Layer 2 - Regional/Divisional Management:

Regional/divisional data access
Team performance monitoring
Target setting and adjustment
Resource allocation

Layer 3 - Branch/Team Managers:

Branch/team customer portfolios
Individual team member activities
Local pipeline management
Cannot access other branches without permission

Layer 4 - Relationship Managers/Sales Staff:

Own customer portfolio only
Assigned leads and prospects
Personal tasks and targets
Limited reporting capabilities

Additional Access Controls:

Role-based permissions (sales, service, support)
Product-line restrictions
Sensitive data masking (complete account numbers, high-net-worth details)
Audit trails for all data access

6. Integration Requirements
Core Banking System (CBS):

Credit scoring and approval systems
